Kobe Bean Bryant was one of the greatest basketball players who ever lived. Drafted 13th overall in the 1996 NBA Draft, Bryant spent his 20-year career with the Los Angeles Lakers, by the end of his career Kobe had 5 NBA Championships, 1 MVP, 2 Finals MVP, 15-time All-NBA Team, and many more awards. Kobe Bryant was took from us away too soon in January 26th in 2020 alongside his daugther Gianna and 7 others. Kobe ispired a generation of basketball players and fans around the world and his impact in their lifes cannot be measured.
